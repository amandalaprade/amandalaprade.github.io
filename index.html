The Wooden Spoon

This name makes me feel nostalgic of my childhood. Growing up with my grandparents and my Father Giacomo- the wooden spoon was a necessity in the kitchen. It was also used as a friendly weapon when we misbehaved.

The people that know me; know I love to EAT and cook! My father always made us taste everything. He was an amazing chef and a beautiful soul.

The Wooden spoon that taught us how to share and experience the taste of food is the art of cooking.
